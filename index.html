Background

The Philippine real estate market is influenced by various factors, including economic growth, infrastructure development, and population density. There is a pressing issue of housing affordability, particularly in urban areas, where real estate prices continue to rise, and making homeownership increasingly inaccessible for many segments of the population. This has led to housing insecurity, forced evictions, informal settlements, and social exclusion, exacerbating inequalities and hindering sustainable urban development.
